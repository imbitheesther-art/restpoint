## Content Guidelines

### Tone of Voice
- Professional yet approachable
- Kenyan context and examples
- Focus on solving real problems
- Data-driven and factual
- Clear, concise language

### Content Structure
1. **Introduction**: Address the pain point
2. **Problem**: Explain the challenge
3. **Solution**: Present Rest Point as the answer
4. **Features**: Detail key functionalities
5. **Benefits**: Show value proposition
6. **Call-to-Action**: Encourage demo/trial

### SEO Best Practices
- Primary keyword in title, H1, meta description
- Secondary keywords in H2, H3 tags
- Natural keyword density (1-2%)
- Internal linking to related pages
- External links to authoritative sources
- Image optimization with alt text
- Schema markup for rich snippets
- Mobile-responsive design
- Fast page load times

## Internal Linking Strategy

### Hub Pages (Pillar Content)
